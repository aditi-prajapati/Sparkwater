About our Spark Water

Spark Water Group is a vertical Company engaged in Real Estate, Design & Interiors, Hospitality and Chemical Manufacturing. In the Real Estate sector Spark Group have been in the business since 1989, and have created many architectural landmarks in Vadodara.

One landmark the Company had always nurtured was to create a lush green riverside project with luxurious facilities that give the feel of a resort while being at home. Hence, the idea of Sparkwater Villas & Duplex Apartments was born. It stands out as a benchmark of quality and innovation. It is a lush green riverside project built on the Mahisagar riverfront dam with picturesque & natural beauty coupled with resort-like facilities for ultra-luxurious stay at affordable prices.

Abounding with latest technology, unique services and located at the right distance, this design is a technical innovation and a wonderful combination of sustainable energy initiatives & wealth in artistic sense.
